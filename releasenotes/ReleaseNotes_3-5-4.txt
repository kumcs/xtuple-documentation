Release Notes
xTuple ERP
PostBooks, Standard, and Manufacturing Editions
Version 3.5.4
September 10, 2010
==================================

This is the patch release for version 3.5.4.

==================================

The following bug fixes have been added to the applications since
the release of 3.5.3. Additional detail for each item listed
below may be found on our community website (www.xtuple.org).
Simply go to the Issue Tracker and select the "Changelog" option.


Bug Fixes:

* [Accounting] The G/L transactions screen's performance is 
significantly less efficient than in 3.2.2
* [Accounting] A/P Workbench doesn't show amounts on multiple 
check selection
* [Accounting] xTuple crashes when mousing over menus
* [Inventory] Decimal precision causes problems when moving 
inventory with UOM conversions
* [Purchase] Division by zero error on purchase price variance 
when porecv_value is zero
* [Sales] Canceling lookup causes crash
* [Sales] Pricing detail ellipses on SO line shows irrelevant 
lines
